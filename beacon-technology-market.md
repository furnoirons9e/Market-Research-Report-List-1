<p><p>Beacon technology utilizes various methods for proximity communication and location-based services. Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) is widely used for its low power consumption and effective range. Wi-Fi beacons leverage existing networks for broader coverage and higher data transfer rates. Ultrasound beacons use sound waves for device detection, providing an alternative approach that can work in noisy environments. Combined technologies integrate these methods to enhance reliability and versatility, catering to diverse applications in retail, healthcare, and smart cities.</p></p>

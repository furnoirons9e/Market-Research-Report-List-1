<p><p>The Automatic Visual Inspection System market is divided into front inspection and back inspection types. Front inspection focuses on assessing the visible aspects of products, ensuring quality and detecting surface defects before they reach consumers. Back inspection, on the other hand, primarily examines components that may not be readily visible, such as internal or structural features, ensuring product integrity and performance. Both types are critical in enhancing manufacturing processes and ensuring product reliability across various industries.</p></p>

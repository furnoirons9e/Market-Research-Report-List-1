<p><p>The artificial tears market comprises two primary types: artificial tear liquids and artificial tear ointments. Artificial tear liquids are water-based solutions designed to provide immediate lubrication and relief from dry eyes. They often come in various formulations, including preservative-free options for sensitive eyes. In contrast, artificial tear ointments are thicker, gel-like substances that provide longer-lasting moisture and protection, making them ideal for overnight use or for more severe dryness. Both types aim to alleviate discomfort and improve eye health.</p></p>

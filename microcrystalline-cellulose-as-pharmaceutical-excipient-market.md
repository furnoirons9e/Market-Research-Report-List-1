The market is divided into two main types: wood pulp-based and refined cotton-based MCC. Wood pulp-based MCC is derived from plant fibers, offering cost-effectiveness and high availability. In contrast, refined cotton-based MCC is produced from high-purity cotton, providing enhanced flow properties and compressibility. Both types cater to different formulations, ensuring efficient drug delivery and stability in pharmaceuticals.</p></p>

<p><p>The surgical nitrile gloves market is primarily divided into two types: powdered and non-powdered gloves. Non-powdered gloves, on the other hand, are free from powder, minimizing allergic reactions and contamination while offering a reliable and secure fit. Both types are crucial for maintaining hygiene and safety in medical environments.</p></p>

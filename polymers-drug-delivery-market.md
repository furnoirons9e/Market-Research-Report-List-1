<p><ul><li>Micelles</li><li>Microgels</li><li>Dendrimer</li><li>Hydrogels</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polymers drug delivery market encompasses various innovative carrier systems. Micelles are nanoscale structures formed by amphiphilic molecules, enhancing drug solubility and stability. Microgels are porous polymer networks capable of swelling, allowing controlled release of therapeutic agents. Dendrimers are branched macromolecules with precise structures, offering targeted delivery and multifunctionality. Hydrogels are hydrophilic polymer matrices that retain water, enabling sustained release and biocompatibility. Other market types may include nanoparticles and liposomes, further advancing drug delivery technologies.</p></p>

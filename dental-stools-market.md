<p><strong>The Dental Stools Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotating Dental Stools</li><li>Non-rotating Dental Stools</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental stools market is categorized into two main types: rotating and non-rotating dental stools. Rotating dental stools feature a swivel mechanism, allowing dental professionals to easily pivot and access various tools and patients without needing to reposition the stool. This enhances efficiency during procedures. In contrast, non-rotating dental stools have a fixed base, providing stability and simplicity, but lack the movement advantages of rotating models. Both types are essential for ensuring comfort and accessibility in dental practice settings.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Airship Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rigid</li><li>Semi Rigid</li><li>Non-rigid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airship market is categorized into three types: rigid, semi-rigid, and non-rigid airships. Rigid airships feature a sturdy frame that maintains their shape, making them suitable for long-distance travel and heavy cargo. Semi-rigid airships have a partial frame that provides some shape support while allowing for flexibility, often used for advertising and surveillance. Non-rigid airships, or blimps, rely solely on internal gas pressure for shape, commonly utilized for aerial photography and leisure activities, due to their lightweight design.</p></p>

<p><p>The Semi-Flexible Cable market comprises two primary types: Type I and Type II. Type I Semi-Flexible Cables are designed for applications requiring moderate flexibility and can bend to fit various configurations while maintaining good electrical conductivity. Type II Semi-Flexible Cables offer enhanced flexibility and are suitable for dynamic applications where frequent movement or bending occurs. Both types cater to diverse industries, including telecommunications, automotive, and aerospace, providing reliable performance in environments with varying space and movement constraints.</p></p>

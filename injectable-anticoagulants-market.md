<p><strong>The Injectable Anticoagulants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The injectable anticoagulants market comprises several types, each serving specific therapeutic needs. Low Molecular Weight Heparins (LMWH) offer targeted anticoagulation with reduced side effects compared to unfractionated heparin, which is used for immediate anticoagulation in various clinical settings. Fibrinolytics, on the other hand, are employed to dissolve blood clots in acute scenarios like heart attacks. Additionally, the "Others" category includes alternative agents such as direct thrombin inhibitors and factor Xa inhibitors, expanding treatment options for patients requiring anticoagulation.</p></p>

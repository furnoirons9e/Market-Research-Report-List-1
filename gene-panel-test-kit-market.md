<p><ul><li>Amplicon-based Approach</li><li>Hybridization-based Approach</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Gene Panel Test Kit market is primarily categorized into two approaches: amplicon-based and hybridization-based. The amplicon-based approach uses PCR amplification to target specific gene regions, enhancing detection sensitivity and specificity. In contrast, the hybridization-based approach relies on complementary binding of probes to nucleic acid sequences, allowing for the simultaneous analysis of multiple genes. Both methods facilitate personalized medicine and targeted therapy by identifying genetic variations associated with specific diseases, thus driving advancements in diagnostics and treatment strategies.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Double Break Isolator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bus Side</li><li>Line Side</li><li>Transfer Bus Side</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Double Break Isolator market can be categorized into three main types: Bus Side, Line Side, and Transfer Bus Side. </p><p>The Bus Side isolators are used to isolate busbars from the network, ensuring safe maintenance. Line Side isolators disconnect the incoming power lines from the equipment, protecting it during servicing. Transfer Bus Side isolators facilitate switching between multiple busbars, enhancing operational flexibility. Each type ensures safety and reliability in electrical systems, catering to specific isolation needs within power networks.</p></p>

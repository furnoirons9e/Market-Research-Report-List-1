<p><strong>The Air Conveyors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Pressure</li><li>Negative Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air conveyors are categorized into positive pressure and negative pressure systems, each serving distinct functions in material handling. Positive pressure air conveyors push products through a duct using compressed air, making them ideal for transporting lightweight items over longer distances. In contrast, negative pressure systems rely on suction to draw materials through the conveyor, which is beneficial for handling small, intricate components while minimizing product damage. Both types enhance efficiency in various industries by leveraging air for transportation without contact.</p></p>

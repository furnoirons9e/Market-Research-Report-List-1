<p><p>Warehouse Management Platforms can be categorized into two main types: On-premises and Cloud-based solutions. On-premises systems are installed locally on a company's servers and offer greater control and customization but require significant investment in hardware and maintenance. In contrast, Cloud-based platforms operate over the internet, providing flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs, as they are managed by third-party providers. This allows businesses to access their WMS from anywhere, facilitating real-time data updates and collaboration across multiple locations.</p></p>

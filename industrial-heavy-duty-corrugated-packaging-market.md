<p><p>The industrial heavy-duty corrugated packaging market is primarily segmented into two layers and three layers. Two-layer corrugated packaging consists of an inner and outer layer, providing basic protection for lighter goods. It is cost-effective and suitable for various applications. In contrast, three-layer corrugated packaging includes an additional fluted layer in between, offering enhanced strength and durability for heavier or fragile items. This design improves cushioning and resistance to impact, making it ideal for shipping and storage in demanding environments.</p></p>

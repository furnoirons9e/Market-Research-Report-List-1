<p><p>The Electrically Active Smart Glass market encompasses various technologies designed to modify light transmission. Suspended Particle Device (SPD) glass uses suspended particles to control opacity. Electrochromic glass changes color in response to an electric charge, offering energy efficiency. Liquid Crystal/Polymer Disperse Liquid Crystal glass adjusts visibility via orientation of liquid crystals. Micro-blinds integrate movable micro-sized blinds for shading. Nanocrystal glass utilizes nanotechnology to achieve energy-saving, variable shading. Each type enhances user comfort and energy efficiency in buildings and vehicles.</p></p>

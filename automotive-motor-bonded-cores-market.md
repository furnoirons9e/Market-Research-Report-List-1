<p><strong>The Automotive Motor Bonded Cores Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Rolled Non-Oriented (CRNO)</li><li>CRGO</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Motor Bonded Cores market primarily consists of two types: Cold Rolled Non-Oriented (CRNO) and Cold Rolled Grain-Oriented (CRGO) materials. CRNO is widely used for applications requiring good magnetic properties in multiple directions, making it suitable for various automotive motors. In contrast, CRGO features enhanced magnetic properties in one direction, optimizing efficiency, particularly in transformers and high-performance motors. Both types are essential for improving energy efficiency and performance in electric and hybrid vehicles.</p></p>

<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Boiler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fire-tube</li><li>Water-tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The boiler market can be classified into two primary types: fire-tube and water-tube boilers. Fire-tube boilers consist of a series of tubes that carry flue gases, surrounded by water, making them suitable for low-pressure applications. They are simpler, cost-effective, and easy to maintain. In contrast, water-tube boilers feature water-filled tubes that are heated by flue gases, allowing for higher pressure and efficiency. They are ideal for large-scale industrial applications and are designed for rapid steam production.</p></p>

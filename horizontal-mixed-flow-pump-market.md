<p><ul><li>Semi-open Impeller</li><li>Open Impeller</li><li>Enclosed Impeller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Horizontal Mixed Flow Pump market features three main types of impellers: semi-open, open, and enclosed. Semi-open impellers allow for some solid passage while maintaining efficiency in flow, making them ideal for applications with small solids. Open impellers, lacking a cover, enhance performance in handling larger solids and debris. Enclosed impellers, fully enclosed between two shrouds, provide high efficiency and flow stability, making them suitable for clean fluids in demanding applications. Each type serves specific industry needs based on operational requirements.</p></p>

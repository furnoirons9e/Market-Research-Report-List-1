<p><p>Additive manufacturing machines can be categorized into several types based on the technology used. Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) extrudes thermoplastic filament layer by layer. Stereolithography (SLA) uses ultraviolet light to curing liquid resin into solid layers. Digital Light Processing (DLP) employs a digital light projector for faster curing. Material Jetting (MJ) sprays droplets of material to build parts. Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) uses a laser to fuse powdered material. Other methods include binder jetting and electron beam melting, catering to diverse applications.</p></p>

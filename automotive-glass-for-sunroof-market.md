<p><p>The automotive glass for sunroofs market includes various types, primarily tempered and laminated glasses. Tempered glass is heat-treated for enhanced strength and safety, shattering into small pieces upon breakage, which reduces injury risk. Laminated glass consists of layers of glass and polyvinyl butyral (PVB), providing higher durability, noise reduction, and UV protection, while remaining intact when fractured. Other materials might include polycarbonate or specialized coatings designed for specific performance features. Each type is selected based on safety, aesthetics, and functionality.</p></p>

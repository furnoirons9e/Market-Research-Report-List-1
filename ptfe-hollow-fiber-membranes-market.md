<p><p>PTFE hollow fiber membranes are categorized into two main types: flat-plate membrane modules and column membrane modules. Flat-plate modules consist of membranes arranged in a flat configuration, allowing for efficient surface area utilization and easy integration into various systems. In contrast, column membrane modules feature hollow fiber membranes housed within a cylindrical structure, optimizing space and enhancing flow dynamics for applications like gas separation and wastewater treatment. Both types offer unique advantages in filtration efficiency and adaptability across diverse industries.</p></p>

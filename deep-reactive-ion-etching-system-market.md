<p><p>The Deep Reactive Ion Etching (DRIE) system market consists primarily of two types: the Bosch process and the Cryo process. The Bosch process alternates between etching and passivation cycles, allowing for precise control over feature depth and profile, making it ideal for microfabrication applications. In contrast, the Cryo process operates at low temperatures, enhancing anisotropic etching and reducing polymer formation, which is beneficial for high aspect ratio structures. Both processes cater to various industries, including semiconductor and MEMS manufacturing.</p></p>

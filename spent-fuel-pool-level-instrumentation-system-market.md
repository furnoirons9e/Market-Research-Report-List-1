<p><strong>The Spent Fuel Pool Level Instrumentation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Guided Wave Radar System</li><li>Through-Air Radar System</li><li>Bubbler System</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Spent Fuel Pool Level Instrumentation System market encompasses various technologies for measuring the water level in spent fuel storage pools. Guided Wave Radar Systems use microwave signals to determine levels by measuring time-of-flight. Through-Air Radar Systems transmit signals through the air, enabling measurements with minimal obstructions. Bubbler Systems involve introducing air into the water, with the pressure correlating to water levels. Other market options may include ultrasonic and capacitive sensors, offering diverse solutions for monitoring pool conditions effectively.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Proton Radiotherapy System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Synchrotron System</li><li>Cyclotron System</li><li>Synchrocyclotron System</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The proton radiotherapy system market is categorized into several types: </p><p>1. **Synchrotron System** - Utilizes high speeds to generate protons, allowing for high energy output and efficient dose delivery.</p><p>2. **Cyclotron System** - Compact and cost-effective, this system accelerates protons using a circular path to provide targeted treatments.</p><p>3. **Synchrocyclotron System** - A variant of cyclotrons, it adjusts the frequency of the magnetic field to accommodate protons of varying energies.</p><p>4. **Others** - This includes emerging technologies and hybrid systems not fitting into the main categories. Each type plays a critical role in advancing cancer treatment.</p></p>

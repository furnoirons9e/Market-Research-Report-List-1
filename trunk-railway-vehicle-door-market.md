Electrical doors utilize automated systems for opening and closing, enhancing efficiency and safety. Pneumatic doors rely on compressed air mechanisms, providing smooth operation and quick response times, often seen in modern trains. Manual doors, operated by hand, are simpler and more traditional, commonly found in older railway vehicles. Each type serves specific operational needs, balancing factors like cost, maintenance, and user convenience.</p></p>

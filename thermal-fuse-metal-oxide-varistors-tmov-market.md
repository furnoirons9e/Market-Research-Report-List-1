<p><p>Thermal Fuse Metal Oxide Varistors (TMOV) are advanced protective components used in electrical circuits to safeguard against voltage transients and surges. This dual functionality makes TMOVs particularly valuable in applications ranging from consumer electronics to industrial equipment.</p><p>The TMOV market is anticipated to experience significant growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 9.00% during the forecast period.

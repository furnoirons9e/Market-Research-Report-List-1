<p><ul><li>Radiofrequency Ablation</li><li>Microwave Ablation</li><li>Cryoablation</li><li>Irreversible Electroporation Ablation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Interventional tumor ablation encompasses various techniques for treating cancerous tumors by destroying them through heat or cold. Radiofrequency ablation uses high-frequency energy to generate heat and kill tumor cells. Microwave ablation employs microwave energy to achieve similar results. Cryoablation utilizes extreme cold to freeze and eliminate tumors. Irreversible electroporation ablation disrupts cell membranes using electrical pulses, leading to cell death. Each method offers unique benefits and is chosen based on tumor type, location, and patient health considerations.</p></p>

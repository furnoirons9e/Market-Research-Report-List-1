<p><p>The Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) Smart Card market is categorized into two main types: contact and contactless. </p><p>Contact smart cards require physical insertion into a reader, providing a secure method for identity verification through a direct connection. Conversely, contactless smart cards utilize radio-frequency identification (RFID) technology, allowing users to authenticate by simply tapping the card near a reader, enhancing convenience and speed. Both types enhance security measures for sensitive transactions and access controls in various sectors, including finance, healthcare, and corporate environments.</p></p>

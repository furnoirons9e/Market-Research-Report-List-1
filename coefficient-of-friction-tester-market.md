<p><ul><li>StaticCoefficientofFriction</li><li>KineticCoefficientofFriction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Coefficient of Friction Tester market includes two main types: Static Coefficient of Friction (SCOF) and Kinetic Coefficient of Friction (KCOF). SCOF measures the frictional force between two surfaces at rest, indicating the amount of force required to initiate movement. In contrast, KCOF assesses the friction between surfaces already in motion, reflecting the resistance encountered during sliding. Both types are crucial for evaluating material performance in various industries, ensuring safety and efficiency in product applications.</p></p>

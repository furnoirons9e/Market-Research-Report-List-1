<p><strong>The Emergency Ventilator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Volume-controlled ventilation modes</li><li>Pressure-controlled ventilation modes</li><li>Spontaneous breathing (non-invasive)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The emergency ventilator market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Volume-controlled ventilation modes** deliver a preset tidal volume, ensuring consistent airflow to patients, which is crucial in critical situations. </p><p>2. **Pressure-controlled ventilation modes** focus on delivering air until a specific pressure is reached, making them suitable for patients with varying lung compliance. </p><p>3. **Spontaneous breathing (non-invasive)** aids patients who can initiate breaths but require support, often using masks to provide assistance without invasive procedures, enhancing comfort and reducing complications.</p></p>

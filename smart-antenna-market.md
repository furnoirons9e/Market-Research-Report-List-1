<p><p>The smart antenna market comprises two primary types: Switched Multi-Beam Antennas and Adaptive Array Antennas. Switched Multi-Beam Antennas utilize multiple fixed beams to enhance coverage and capacity by dynamically directing signals to specific areas, improving connectivity in dense environments. In contrast, Adaptive Array Antennas adjust their radiation patterns in real-time based on signal conditions, optimizing performance and reducing interference. Both types enhance wireless communication efficiency, making them vital in sectors like telecommunications, aerospace, and defense.</p></p>

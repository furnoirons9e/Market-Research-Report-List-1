<p><p>The Temperature Limiting Fuse market consists of two primary types: Fixed Operating Temperature and Thermal Gradient Operating Temperature fuses. Fixed Operating Temperature fuses activate at a specific temperature, providing reliable protection in applications where a consistent overheating threshold is critical. In contrast, Thermal Gradient Operating Temperature fuses respond to temperature variations within a specific area, allowing for more adaptive protection based on environmental changes. Both types serve essential roles in safeguarding electrical systems from overheating and potential damage.</p></p>

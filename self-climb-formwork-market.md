<p><ul><li>Electric Jack Climbing Formwork</li><li>Hydraulic Jack Climbing Formwork</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-climb formwork market comprises two primary types: electric jack climbing formwork and hydraulic jack climbing formwork. Electric jack climbing formwork relies on electric motors to lift and position the formwork system, offering precise control and efficiency. In contrast, hydraulic jack climbing formwork utilizes hydraulic systems for lifting, providing faster setup and greater load capacity. Both types enhance construction productivity and safety, allowing for rapid vertical construction in high-rise buildings while minimizing labor requirements and construction time.</p></p>
